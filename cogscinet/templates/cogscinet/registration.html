CogSci Network Membership

 

Keep the CogSci spirit alive!

In the summer of 2022, the old "Association of Friends of Sponsors of the Institute of Cognitive Science" (F²KW) is either to be closed down or to be transformed into an alumni network that is more closely tailored to the interests of current and former students.

What can such an CogSci network do? These are some goals and plans we have:

  • Create and support a reliable, long-term platform for exchange of ideas and networking among students, staff and alumni.
  • Support events connecting students and alumni, such as the alumni conference.
  • Support students in gaining professional and scientific experiences, e.g. by visting conferences or publishing a Student Journal.
  • Promote student initiatives to open up innovative areas of cognitive science education and training
  • Establish a permanent office for the association to guarantee a constant flow of activities and a permanent contact partner ("450€ job")

For this transformation to be possible, we need a "critical mass" of interested people who are willing to become (or stay) members and to suport the work of the association with their membership fee.

Our aim is to reach an annual revenue of at least 10,000 € from membership fees and donations to be able to pursuit these goals.

Data Privacy

English summary:

All data collected here is received and processed by board members of the F²IKW (Verein der Freunde und Förderer des Instituts für Kognitionswissenschaft e.V.) and their authorized agents only.
